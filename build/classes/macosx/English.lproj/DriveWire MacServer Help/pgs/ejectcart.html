Ejecting a cartridge

Ejecting a cartridge from a virtual disk drive empties the virtual disk drive, allowing another cartridge to be inserted.

To eject a cartridge:

  1. Click on the rectangular button below the cartridge slot area on the face of the virtual disk drive.
  2. When the disk ejects, a sound is emitted and the cartridge is ejected. The virtual disk drive door closes and is ready for another cartridge to be inserted.
