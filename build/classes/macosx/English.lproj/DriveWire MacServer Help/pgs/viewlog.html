DriveWire MacServer Help

Viewing statistics and logs

DriveWire MacServer allows the viewing of both communications statistics and transaction logs.

To view statistics:

  1. Click the Statistics checkbox on the DriveWire MacServer window.
  2. A drawer will appear attached to the window that displays the statistics information for that server.

To view the log:

  1. Click the Log checkbox on the DriveWire MacServer window.
  2. A drawer will appear attached to the window that displays the log for that server.

    You can type annotations in the log if you would like. You may also clear the contents of the log window or copy it to the clipboard by clicking the respective buttons.
